# Incident Response Mini Playbook

## Scenario
A staff member clicks on a phishing email that deploys ransomware on a clinic workstation.

---

## Immediate Actions

### 1. Isolate the affected device
- Disconnect the workstation from the network
- Disable Wi-Fi and VPN access
- Prevent lateral movement

### 2. Notify internal personnel
- Inform clinic management
- Contact outsourced IT/security provider
- Activate incident response procedures

### 3. Preserve evidence
- Do not immediately power off the system
- Preserve logs if possible
- Record affected systems and timeline

### 4. Assess operational impact
- Determine if EHR systems are affected
- Identify disrupted clinical workflows
- Evaluate patient safety risks

### 5. Begin containment and recovery
- Restore from backups if available
- Reset compromised credentials
- Patch vulnerable systems

### 6. External reporting
- Contact law enforcement if necessary
- Evaluate HIPAA breach notification obligations
- Notify affected parties if required

---

## Lessons Learned
- Improve phishing awareness training
- Strengthen MFA adoption
- Validate backup recovery procedures
- Review network segmentation effectiveness
