## Practical Mitigation Matrix
| Threat | Healthcare Impact | Practical Mitigations |
|---|---|---|
| Social Engineering | Credential theft and unauthorized EHR access | MFA, phishing awareness training, email filtering |
| Ransomware | Disrupted care and inaccessible patient records | Offline backups, patching, EDR, incident response plan |
| Loss or Theft of Equipment or Data | Exposure of ePHI | Full-disk encryption, remote wipe, screen locks |
| Insider, Accidental or Malicious Data Loss | Accidental or unauthorized disclosure and misuse of patient data | Least privilege, logging, access reviews |
| Attacks against Network Connected Medical Devices | Disruption of connected medical systems | Network segmentation, asset inventory, firmware updates |
